Diseases & Conditions
Easy-to-understand answers about diseases and conditions

Diseases Starting with Q

1. Quinsy

Symptoms: Severe sore throat, difficulty swallowing, fever, swollen tonsils.

Definition: Quinsy, also known as peritonsillar abscess, is a complication of tonsillitis where a collection of pus forms between the tonsil and the throat wall.

2. Q fever

Symptoms: High fever, headache, muscle pain, cough, fatigue.

Definition: Q fever is a bacterial infection caused by Coxiella burnetii which can cause acute or chronic illness, usually transmitted to humans from animals.

3. Quinine poisoning

Symptoms: Ringing in the ears (tinnitus), headache, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, visual disturbances.

Definition: Quinine poisoning occurs when someone takes too much quinine, a medication used to treat malaria, leading to toxic effects on the body.

4. Quiescent lupus erythematosus

Symptoms: Periods of remission with few or no symptoms, followed by flare-ups of symptoms such as joint pain, fatigue, skin rashes.

Definition: Quiescent lupus erythematosus refers to a period of inactivity or remission in the symptoms of systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE).

5. Qazi-Markouizos syndrome

Symptoms: Intellectual disability, seizures, speech delay, behavioral problems.

Definition: Qazi-Markouizos syndrome is a rare genetic disorder characterized by developmental delay, intellectual disability, seizures, and distinctive facial features.

6. Qazi-Markouizos-like syndrome

Symptoms: Intellectual disability, seizures, speech delay, behavioral problems, distinctive facial features.

Definition: Qazi-Markouizos-like syndrome shares similarities with Qazi-Markouizos syndrome, but the specific genetic cause may be different.

7. Qazi–Markouizos syndrome

Symptoms: Intellectual disability, seizures, speech delay, behavioral problems, distinctive facial features.

Definition: Qazi-Markouizos syndrome is a rare genetic disorder characterized by developmental delay, intellectual disability, seizures, and distinctive facial features.

8. Qazi–Markouizos-like syndrome

Symptoms: Intellectual disability, seizures, speech delay, behavioral problems, distinctive facial features.

Definition: Qazi-Markouizos-like syndrome shares similarities with Qazi-Markouizos syndrome, but the specific genetic cause may be different.

9. Quetzalcoatlus

Symptoms: N/A (This is a genus of pterosaur, not a disease)

Definition: Quetzalcoatlus is a genus of pterosaur known from the Late Cretaceous period of North America. It was one of the largest known flying animals of all time.

10. Quillback

Symptoms: N/A (This is a species of fish, not a disease)

Definition: Quillback is a species of fish native to North America, commonly found in freshwater rivers and lakes.

11. Quinism

Symptoms: Tinnitus, hearing loss, vertigo, headache, nausea, visual disturbances.

Definition: Quinism refers to the toxic effects caused by exposure to quinoline-containing medications, such as mefloquine or quinine.

12. Q fever endocarditis

Symptoms: Fever, fatigue, night sweats, weight loss, shortness of breath, chest pain.

Definition: Q fever endocarditis is a complication of Q fever where the infection affects the heart valves, leading to inflammation and damage.

13. Quadrantanopia

Symptoms: Loss of vision in one quarter of the visual field, usually affecting one eye.

Definition: Quadrantanopia is a type of visual field defect where there is loss of vision in one quarter of the visual field.

14. Quadriparesis

Symptoms: Weakness or partial paralysis affecting all four limbs.

Definition: Quadriparesis is a condition characterized by weakness or partial paralysis affecting all four limbs.

15. Quadriplegia

Symptoms: Paralysis affecting all four limbs and typically the trunk.

Definition: Quadriplegia, also known as tetraplegia, is paralysis caused by illness or injury that results in the partial or total loss of use of all four limbs and the torso.

16. Quail disease

Symptoms: N/A (This is a viral disease affecting quail, not humans)

Definition: Quail disease, also known as avian leukosis, is a viral disease affecting quail and other poultry.

17. Quartz pneumoconiosis

Symptoms: Cough, shortness of breath, chest tightness, chest pain.

Definition: Quartz pneumoconiosis, also known as silicosis, is a lung disease caused by inhaling silica dust over a long period, leading to inflammation and scarring of lung tissue.

18. Queen's disease

Symptoms: N/A (This term might refer to various conditions, not a specific disease)

Definition: The term "Queen's disease" is not commonly used in medical literature and may refer to different conditions depending on the context.

19. Quickening

Symptoms: Sensation of fetal movements during pregnancy.

Definition: Quickening is the term used to describe the sensation of fetal movements felt by a pregnant woman, usually starting between 16 to 25 weeks of pregnancy.

20. Q-fever meningitis

Symptoms: Headache, fever, stiff neck, nausea, vomiting.

Definition: Q-fever meningitis is a rare complication of Q fever where the infection spreads to the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord, causing inflammation.
