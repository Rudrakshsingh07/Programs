Diseases & Conditions
Easy-to-understand answers about diseases and conditions

Diseases Starting with Y

Yellow Fever
Symptoms: Fever, muscle pain, headache, jaundice, vomiting, and fatigue.
Definition: A viral hemorrhagic fever transmitted by infected mosquitoes, causing fever and jaundice, and frequently fatal if severe.
Yersinia enterocolitica Infection
Symptoms: Fever, abdominal pain, diarrhea, nausea, and vomiting.
Definition: A bacterial infection caused by Yersinia enterocolitica, often resulting in gastrointestinal symptoms.
Yaws
Symptoms: Skin lesions, bone and joint pain, swollen lymph nodes.
Definition: A chronic bacterial infection caused by Treponema pallidum subspecies pertenue, transmitted by direct contact with infectious lesions.
Yersiniosis
Symptoms: Diarrhea, fever, abdominal pain, vomiting, and occasionally, a rash.
Definition: An infection caused by bacteria of the genus Yersinia, typically transmitted through contaminated food or water.
Yersinia pestis Infection (Plague)
Symptoms: Fever, chills, weakness, swollen and painful lymph nodes, shortness of breath, cough.
Definition: A serious bacterial infection caused by Yersinia pestis, usually spread through fleas from rodents.
Yellow Nail Syndrome
Symptoms: Yellowish discoloration of nails, lymphedema (swelling), respiratory symptoms.
Definition: A rare disorder characterized by yellow nails, respiratory problems, and lymphedema.
Yolk Sac Tumor
Symptoms: Abdominal swelling or mass, pain, fever, weight loss.
Definition: A rare malignant tumor that primarily occurs in children, arising from germ cells in the yolk sac of the embryo.
Yaba Monkey Tumor Virus (YMTV) Infection
Symptoms: Swelling and formation of tumors, fever, respiratory symptoms.
Definition: A virus belonging to the herpesvirus family, causing tumors in monkeys.
