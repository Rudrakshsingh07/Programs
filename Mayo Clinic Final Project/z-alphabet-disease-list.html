Diseases & Conditions
Easy-to-understand answers about diseases and conditions

Diseases Starting with Z

Zika Virus
Symptoms: Fever, rash, joint pain, conjunctivitis, muscle pain, headache.
Definition: Zika virus is a mosquito-borne virus transmitted primarily by Aedes mosquitoes, which bite during the day.
Zollinger-Ellison Syndrome
Symptoms: Abdominal pain, diarrhea, acid reflux, ulcers, unintended weight loss.
Definition: Zollinger-Ellison Syndrome is a rare condition characterized by tumors in the pancreas or duodenum that secrete excessive levels of gastrin, leading to the overproduction of stomach acid and ulcers.
Zygomycosis
Symptoms: Fever, headache, cough, chest pain, shortness of breath, skin lesions, black necrotic areas on skin.
Definition: Zygomycosis, also known as mucormycosis, is a serious fungal infection caused by fungi of the order Mucorales. It primarily affects people with weakened immune systems.
Zellweger Syndrome
Symptoms: Facial abnormalities, severe intellectual disability, poor muscle tone, liver dysfunction, hearing and vision problems.
Definition: Zellweger Syndrome is a rare genetic disorder characterized by the absence or dysfunction of peroxisomes in cells, resulting in the accumulation of toxic substances in the body.
Zinc Deficiency
Symptoms: Growth retardation, delayed sexual maturation, impaired immune function, hair loss, diarrhea, delayed wound healing.
Definition: Zinc deficiency is a nutritional disorder caused by inadequate intake or absorption of zinc, an essential mineral involved in various physiological processes.
Zygotene Stage of Meiosis
Symptoms: Not applicable (as it's a stage of cellular division).
Definition: The zygotene stage is the second stage of meiosis I, during which homologous chromosomes begin to pair and synapse, forming structures called synaptonemal complexes.
Zoonosis
Symptoms: Varies depending on the specific zoonotic disease (e.g., fever, rash, respiratory symptoms, gastrointestinal symptoms).
Definition: Zoonosis is any infectious disease that can be transmitted from animals to humans, either directly or indirectly.
Zytochrome P450 Deficiency
Symptoms: Metabolic disturbances, drug toxicity, hormone imbalances, jaundice, virilization in females.
Definition: Cytochrome P450 deficiency is a group of genetic disorders affecting enzymes in the cytochrome P450 family, which play a crucial role in the metabolism of drugs and other substances in the body.
Zinc Toxicity
Symptoms: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal pain, headache, metallic taste, lethargy.
Definition: Zinc toxicity, or zinc overdose, occurs when there is an excessive intake or accumulation of zinc in the body, leading to adverse effects on health.
Zygote Intrafallopian Transfer (ZIFT)
Symptoms: Not applicable (it's a reproductive procedure).
Definition: Zygote intrafallopian transfer (ZIFT) is an assisted reproductive technology procedure where a fertilized egg (zygote) is transferred into the fallopian tube to facilitate implantation and pregnancy.
