Diseases & Conditions
Easy-to-understand answers about diseases and conditions

Diseases Starting with O

Osteoarthritis
A common joint disorder due to aging and wear and tear on a joint.
Obesity
Excessive body fat that increases the risk of health problems.
Osteoporosis
A condition characterized by weak and brittle bones, increasing the risk of fractures.
Ovarian Cancer
Cancer that forms in tissues of the ovary. It is often undetected until it has spread.
Oral Thrush
An infection caused by the Candida fungus, resulting in white lesions on the tongue and inner cheeks.
Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
A mental health disorder characterized by obsessive thoughts and compulsive behaviors.
Obstructive Sleep Apnea
A sleep disorder characterized by pauses in breathing during sleep due to obstruction of the airway.
Osteomyelitis
An infection of the bone usually caused by bacteria or fungi.
Organic Brain Syndrome
A general term for physical disorders impairing brain function, leading to cognitive and behavioral symptoms.
Onychomycosis
A fungal infection of the nails, causing them to become thickened, discolored, and brittle.
Ovarian Cyst
A fluid-filled sac that forms on the ovary. It is usually harmless but can sometimes cause pain or complications.
Otitis Media
An infection or inflammation of the middle ear, often causing pain and fluid buildup.
Ocular Hypertension
Higher than normal pressure inside the eye, which can lead to glaucoma if left untreated.
Oral Cancer
Cancer that develops in the tissues of the mouth or throat, often associated with tobacco and alcohol use.
Omphalitis
An infection of the umbilical stump in newborns, usually caused by bacteria.
Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)
A childhood behavioral disorder characterized by defiance, hostility, and disobedience towards authority figures.
Orthostatic Hypotension
A sudden drop in blood pressure when standing up, leading to dizziness or fainting.
Osteogenesis Imperfecta
Also known as brittle bone disease, a genetic disorder characterized by fragile bones that break easily.
Overactive Bladder
A condition characterized by frequent and sudden urges to urinate.
Orchitis
Inflammation of one or both testicles, often caused by a viral or bacterial infection.
Osler-Weber-Rendu Disease (Hereditary Hemorrhagic Telangiectasia)
A genetic disorder characterized by abnormal blood vessel formation, leading to recurrent nosebleeds and other symptoms.
Occupational Asthma
A type of asthma triggered by exposure to workplace irritants or allergens.
Olfactory Reference Syndrome
A psychiatric disorder characterized by an irrational belief that one emits a foul odor, despite evidence to the contrary.
Ornithosis
An infectious disease caused by the bacterium Chlamydia psittaci, transmitted to humans from birds, especially parrots.
Oculopharyngeal Muscular Dystrophy
A genetic disorder characterized by weakness and wasting of certain muscles, particularly in the eyes and throat.
Osgood-Schlatter Disease
A condition causing pain and swelling below the knee in adolescents, particularly those who are physically active.
Ovarian Torsion
A medical emergency where the ovary twists on its supporting tissues, cutting off its blood supply.
Orbital Cellulitis
An infection of the tissues surrounding the eye, often caused by bacteria spreading from nearby structures.
Occipital Neuralgia
A condition characterized by chronic pain in the back of the head and neck, often radiating to the eyes and forehead.
