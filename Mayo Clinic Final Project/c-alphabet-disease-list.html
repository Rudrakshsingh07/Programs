Diseases & Conditions
Easy-to-understand answers about diseases and conditions

Diseases Starting with C

Cancer
A disease characterized by abnormal cell growth with the potential to invade or spread to other parts of the body. Symptoms may include unexplained weight loss, persistent fatigue, and changes in bowel or bladder habits.
Common Cold
A viral infection affecting the nose and throat. Symptoms typically include coughing, sneezing, sore throat, and runny or stuffy nose.
Cholera
An infectious disease caused by the bacterium Vibrio cholerae, resulting in severe diarrhea and dehydration. It is often transmitted through contaminated food or water.
Chickenpox
An infectious disease caused by the varicella-zoster virus. Symptoms include an itchy rash, fever, and headache.
Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)
A group of lung diseases that block airflow and make breathing difficult. Symptoms may include chronic cough, shortness of breath, and wheezing.
Celiac Disease
An autoimmune disorder in which the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Symptoms may include diarrhea, abdominal pain, and fatigue.
Cardiovascular Disease
A class of diseases that involve the heart or blood vessels. Symptoms may vary depending on the specific condition but can include chest pain, shortness of breath, and dizziness.
Conjunctivitis
Also known as pink eye, it is an inflammation of the conjunctiva, the transparent membrane that covers the white part of the eye and lines the inner surface of the eyelids. Symptoms include redness, itchiness, and discharge from the eye.
Crohn's Disease
A chronic inflammatory bowel disease that affects the lining of the digestive tract. Symptoms may include abdominal pain, diarrhea, and fatigue.
Cirrhosis
A late stage of scarring (fibrosis) of the liver caused by many forms of liver diseases and conditions, such as hepatitis and chronic alcoholism. Symptoms may include fatigue, easy bruising, and jaundice.
Cushing's Syndrome
A hormonal disorder caused by prolonged exposure to high levels of the hormone cortisol. Symptoms may include weight gain, high blood pressure, and muscle weakness.
Chlamydia
A common sexually transmitted infection caused by the bacterium Chlamydia trachomatis. Symptoms may include genital discharge, burning during urination, and pelvic pain.
Cystic Fibrosis
A hereditary disorder that affects the lungs and digestive system. Symptoms may include persistent cough with thick mucus, recurrent lung infections, and poor growth.
Cerebral Palsy
A group of disorders that affect movement and muscle coordination. Symptoms may vary but can include muscle stiffness, poor coordination, and tremors.
Coronary Artery Disease
A condition caused by the buildup of plaque in the walls of the coronary arteries, which supply blood to the heart muscle. Symptoms may include chest pain (angina), shortness of breath, and heart attack.
Cataracts
A clouding of the lens in the eye that affects vision. Symptoms may include blurry vision, difficulty seeing at night, and sensitivity to glare.
Celiac Disease
An autoimmune disorder in which the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. Symptoms may include diarrhea, abdominal pain, and fatigue.
Colitis
An inflammation of the colon. Symptoms may include abdominal pain, diarrhea, and rectal bleeding.
Chronic Kidney Disease
A progressive loss of kidney function over time. Symptoms may include fatigue, swelling, and changes in urination.
Crohn's Disease
A chronic inflammatory bowel disease that affects the lining of the digestive tract. Symptoms may include abdominal pain, diarrhea, and fatigue.
Cystitis
An inflammation of the bladder, often caused by a bacterial infection. Symptoms may include urinary urgency, frequent urination, and pelvic discomfort.
Cholecystitis
An inflammation of the gallbladder, usually caused by gallstones. Symptoms may include abdominal pain, nausea, and fever.
Chronic Fatigue Syndrome
A complex disorder characterized by extreme fatigue that cannot be explained by any underlying medical condition. Other symptoms may include muscle pain, impaired memory, and headaches.
